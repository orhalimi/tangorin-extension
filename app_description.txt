Display tangorin example sentence in Anki friendly format.

For Japanese learner.

On http://tangorin.com/examples/* you will have a new red button near each example sentence. Clicking on it will find the searched word in it and create a three cards format.

The first format will include the question and the sentence
The second format will include the sentence with anki furigana format (without the searched word).
The third format will include the word, word hiragana, and the translation of the word and the exampled sentence.
